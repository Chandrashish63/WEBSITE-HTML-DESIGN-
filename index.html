ABOUT US

Transit Inn is an excellent choice for travellers visiting Kolkata (Calcutta), offering a budget friendly environment alongside many helpful amenities designed to enhance your stay. Close to some of Kolkata (Calcutta)'s most popular landmarks, such as Lake Town Clock Tower (1.4 mi) and Netaji Indoor Stadium (1.8 mi), Transit Inn is a great destination for tourists.
As you are home away from home, the inn rooms offer air conditioning, a desk, and a seating area, and getting online is easy, with wifi available..

Guests have access to 24 hour front desk, room service, and 24 hour security while staying at Transit Inn.

While you are here, be sure to check out some of the barbeque restaurants, including Kebabish Grill India, The Steak Factory, and Grill Mates, all of which are a short distance from Transit Inn.

If you are looking for things to do, you can check out Baba Vishwanath Mandir (0.5 mi), which is a popular attraction amongst tourists, and it is within walking distance. Enjoy your stay in Kolkata (Calcutta)!
